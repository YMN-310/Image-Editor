Choose Your Model!

Instructions:

  • 1. Select a model by clicking on it. A new page will open with the selected model.
  • 2. Select the preferred view by dragging in any direction. Use mouse scroll to zoom-in or zoom-out.
  • 3. Click on start editing button. A new page will open with image editor.
    • a. Click on menu button on top right corner to access different tools.
    • b. Finally, click on download button to save the edited image on your device.
